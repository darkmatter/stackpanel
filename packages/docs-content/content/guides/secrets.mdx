---
title: Secrets
description: Stackpanel secrets with direct SOPS recipients
---

Stackpanel secrets are stored in SOPS files under `vars/` and encrypted directly to the public keys configured in Nix.

- No extra group private keys are created.
- Groups like `dev` and `prod` are just file buckets.
- Configure recipients under `stackpanel.secrets.recipients` (or `stackpanel.users`).
- Use `.stack/secrets/bin/rekey.sh` after recipient changes.
